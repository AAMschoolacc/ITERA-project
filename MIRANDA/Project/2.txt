<p align=center>
<font face="Agency FB" size=7 color="yellow"> DID YOU KNOW? </p>

The moon has no atmosphere, which means it has no protection from cosmic rays, meteorites, and solar winds. It also has no sound and a black sky, since there is no air to scatter light. The temperature on the moon varies greatly, from -233°C to 123°C, depending on whether it is exposed to the sun or not.
